# Weekly Review Profile

**When**: Every Sunday, ~30 minutes
**Goal**: Close the week cleanly, open the next week with clarity

## Checklist

- [ ] Read daily logs from the past 7 days
- [ ] Fill in `_codex/templates/review.md` → save to `HP/29 Life Systems/Reviews/`
- [ ] Run inbox triage (see `_codex/skills/organize.md`)
- [ ] Update status on all active projects
- [ ] Review `_codex/system/active-goals.md` — are priorities still right?
- [ ] Set top 3 priorities for next week → add to next week's first daily log

## The 3 Questions

1. What actually happened this week (vs. what I planned)?
2. What am I carrying into next week that I should drop?
3. What's the single most important thing next week?
