# HP — Holistic Performance (25–49)

> *Optimize the human running the system.*

---

## What This Quadrant Is

**Holistic Performance** is your personal domain — everything that makes *you* function at your best. Your body, your mind, your money, your relationships, your daily systems. This is where you manage yourself.

Most productivity systems stop here. One Mind goes further — but without a solid HP foundation, nothing in LE or GE can be built sustainably.

---

## The Analogy

```text
HP = The person (hardware + software + fuel)
SO = The brain
LE = The home
GE = The mission
```

## My Approach (Zeus's Example)

HP is where I do the most *personal* work. My `25 Identity` folder holds my core values, my life philosophy, and the big-picture vision for who I'm becoming. My `29 Life Systems` has every routine, habit stack, and daily operating procedure.

I track my physical metrics in `27 Body`, run personal finance reviews in `30 Finance` (monthly), and maintain relationship notes in `32 Relationships` so I never forget important context about the people in my life.

**Biggest lesson:** Your identity and your systems need to be written down. What you leave in your head gets lost. What you write in HP becomes programmable — your AI can help you maintain it.

---

## Getting Started

1. Start in `25 Identity` — write down your core values and your current life vision (even rough)
2. Build a simple routine in `29 Life Systems` — even just a morning checklist
3. Capture your financial snapshot in `30 Finance`
4. Add people who matter to you in `32 Relationships`
