# Family Communication Protocol

## Purpose

Keep family communication structured without making it feel corporate. The goal is connection, not process.

## Weekly Family Meeting

- **When**: Sunday evening, 30 minutes max
- **Where**: Dining table, devices away
- **Agenda**:
  1. Wins — each person shares one highlight from the week
  2. Calendar — review the upcoming week together
  3. Needs — anything anyone needs help with
  4. Fun — plan one fun thing for the week

## Shared Calendar Rules

- All family events go in the shared calendar (not personal calendars)
- 24-hour notice minimum for schedule changes
- Color coding: Family (green), Kids (blue), Work (red), Health (yellow)

## Emergency Contacts

Stored in `57 Legal (Documents)/` — emergency contact list, insurance info, medical info for each family member.

## How the Haven Agent Helps

Haven monitors the shared calendar and sends morning briefings. If there's a conflict (two events at the same time), Haven flags it before it becomes a problem.
