# GE — Generational Entrepreneurship (75–99)

> *Build something that outlasts you.*

---

## What This Quadrant Is

**Generational Entrepreneurship** is your business and wealth domain — everything you're building professionally, financially, and entrepreneurially. Personal brand, ventures, products, strategies, partnerships.

The word "generational" is intentional. This isn't about quick wins. It's about architecting wealth and impact that compounds over decades and transfers to the next generation.

## My Approach (Zeus's Example)

My GE quadrant centers on one core model: **Zeus Delacruz teaches the One Mind framework** the way Tiago Forte teaches Building a Second Brain. Domain `75 Personal Brand` holds my brand architecture, content strategy, and speaking frameworks.

Domain `76 Education` is where the community business lives — One Mind Codex as a course and membership. Domain `84 Ventures` is where I incubate new ideas before they become real businesses.

The key discipline: **everything in GE links back to my identity in HP (`25 Identity`) and my values**. Business decisions that conflict with my identity get vetoed, no matter the financial upside.

## Getting Started

1. Start with `75 Personal Brand` — document who you are publicly and what you're building
2. Map your business model in `81 Strategy`, even just a one-pager
3. Track your business finances in `80 Treasury` from day one
4. Capture venture ideas in `84 Ventures` so they don't die in your head
